Companion Edit Reasons ?
Companion Edit Reasons The list of additional files that would be impacted (directly or via shadow coupling) if you make the proposed change. Includes confidence scores and the reason (import, co-change, etc.).

Why useful: Shows the full blast radius so you don't miss hidden dependencies. Essential for safe refactoring and for the Foundry agent to understand the real scope.
Evidence Explorer ?
Evidence Explorer A list of all affected modules/nodes from the simulation, with their type (target/static/shadow), complexity, imports, and commit frequency. Pulled directly from the real nodes in the blast radius calculation.

Why useful: Quick reference for every file touched by the counterfactual change. Helps verify the graph and plan against actual data from the repo.
Simulation Exceptions & Risks ?
Simulation Exceptions & Risks The list of architectural risks and potential issues detected for the impacted files (e.g. god objects, circular deps, missing tests). Comes from the RiskEngine with severity, evidence, and confidence.

Why useful: Surfaces problems that the proposed change could exacerbate or that already exist in the blast radius. Critical for the "Safety gates checked" step and for producing a trustworthy plan.
